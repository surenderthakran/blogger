About The Author

My name is Surender Thakran. I have been working with web technologies since early 2013 and managing code architectures since early 2014 so that makes me sort of psuedo veteran to the paradigm. I have mostly been associated with small to medium sized new age startups which gave me a substantial understanding of ins and outs of a complete product development process.

I am a tech enthusiast who likes to play with all kinds of web technologies in my free time. I intend this blog to be record of my explorations with technology outside of work. In the long run I would be adding a number of articles and posts about implementations of different types of technologies and how to gel them together to form a stable process chain.

Note: The project is a work in progress and is in very early stages of development. You can checkout the progress on the blog on its GitHub Repo.
